## Tutorial on Adding Heat Set Inserts 
 ### [Video Tutorial](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P7nHyI1TwKY)
### Tips
* This is inserting metal threads into your 3D prints to strengthen those connections.
* The iron will have different tips that can be attatched to it. Use a tip that is slightly smaller than your insert so that it will not get stuck. 
* Allow the saudering iron to heat up before attempting to insert. The insert should easily melt into the print with hardly any force.
